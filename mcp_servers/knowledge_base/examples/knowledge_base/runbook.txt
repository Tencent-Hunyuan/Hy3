If SQLite reports that FTS5 or the trigram tokenizer is unavailable, stop indexing and
report the missing capability. Do not silently fall back to an unbounded full scan.
Exact two-word action: Stop indexing.

If the Hy3 endpoint returns HTTP 429, wait at least 60 seconds before retrying remote
generation. Local hy3_kb_search and hy3_kb_list_sources remain available.
